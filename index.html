A Study of the Methods of Solutions

All students can become creators when they learn how to approach problems by understanding what is the unknown, what is the data and how the problem happens.  Once the problem is understood, than a plan can be put together that uses the relations between the known parts of the problem and the unknown.  Carry out the plan, checking each step.   Finally, look back at the result - check it.  Can you use the result or method for other problems?
